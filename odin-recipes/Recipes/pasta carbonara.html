Pasta Carbonara

Description of the dish

Pasta alla carbonara or spaghetti alla carbonara is a pasta dish that is part of Italian cuisine.

The dish consists of pork in the form of guanciale or pancetta, egg, pecorino, black pepper and pasta, often spaghetti.

Ingredients
  • 1/2 package spaghetti
  • 1 package diced guanciale
  • 2 garlic cloves finely chopped
  • 4 egg yolks
  • 85 grams pecorino romano coarsely grated
  • 4 tbsp extra virgin olive oil classico
  • Salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
Steps
  1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions and save 1 1/2 dl of the cooking water
  2. Pour a little olive oil into a frying pan and crisp fry the guanciale
  3. Add the garlic towards the end
  4. Whisk together egg yolks and grated cheese in a bowl, mix with 2â€“3 tablespoons of cooled pasta water
  5. Reduce to very low heat on the stove.Turn the freshly cooked pasta into the frying pan and stir in the egg and cheese mixture.It is very important that the pan is not too hot, then the egg will harden and the sauce will become firm and grainy
  6. Thin with pasta water to achieve a creamy consistency
  7. Season with salt
  8. Grind well with black pepper, so that it looks almost like grains of coal
  9. Serve with a little more pecorino or parmesan cheese and a few drops of olive oil on top of each portion
  10. Some like to garnish with a little parsley
